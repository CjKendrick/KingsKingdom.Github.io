Our Story

Kings was started by a African American family striving to better their community by providing a upscale fine dining restaraunt. A place where people could come and feel like someone important all while eating succulent tastful food. Kings was opened in 2020 and has been the pinnacle of fine dining in New York ever since being showcased in Magazines and spoken of highly by accredited food critics.
